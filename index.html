Hadley Leggett
Writer, reader, mother of three.

About

Hadley lives and writes in Seattle, WA. Her winding career path has included degrees in medicine, biochemistry, and science journalism, before she finally found her home in fiction.

When not chasing after her three children, Hadley is working on the final draft of her first novel. Her work has appeared at Literary Mama, 50-Word Stories, and Wired.com.
