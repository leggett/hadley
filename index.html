    All They Ask Is Everything


    Three women all trying to be the perfect mom. For the same two little girls.

    ALL THE ASK IS EVERYTHING is the story of a fiercely independent young widow whose devastating mistake lands her daughters in foster care and forces her to accept help from the last two women she wants to rely on: her estranged mother and the girls' new foster mom, who each want custody for their own complicated reasons. Told from the divergent perspectives of the three women fighting for custody, the book explores what it means to be a mother in a society that demands everything of parents but offers little in the way of support.
